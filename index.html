Welcome to AnVira

A Collection Built
on Restraint

AnVira is not a catalogue. It is a considered edit — three private estates across India, each selected for its sense of place, its architecture, and the quality of stillness it offers to those who stay.

From the pine ridges of Chail to the riverside calm of Goa and the curated seclusion of New Delhi, every property carries a story. We exist to connect you to that story — and nothing more.

The AnVira Experience
What every stay is shaped around
Private by Design
Entire estates reserved exclusively for your group — no shared amenities, no strangers, no compromise on intimacy.
Unhurried Rhythm
Flexible arrivals, generous timings, and a pace set entirely by you — because a true retreat answers to no itinerary but yours.
Dedicated Staffing
Each property comes with in-house caretaking staff who know it intimately — discreet, attentive, and fluent in anticipating needs.
Curated Interiors
Thoughtfully furnished spaces where each piece has been placed with intention — art, textiles, and objects that speak to the local landscape.
Security & Privacy
Gated access, fully walled grounds, and complete guest discretion — your presence at our estates remains entirely your own business.
White-Glove Support
A single point of contact from first enquiry to check-out — handling every arrangement so your only task is to arrive and unwind.

The Philosophy
